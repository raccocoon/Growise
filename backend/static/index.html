🌱 What to Plant

AI recommends the top 5 crops based on your farm profile, soil type, and current 6-day weather. Powered by Groq AI via OpenRouter.

📖 How to Plant

Select a crop from your recommendations to get a complete personalized planting guide. AI adapts the guide to your experience level.

📅 When to Plant

Scores each of the 30 days to find the best consecutive planting windows. Days 1–6 use real forecast. Days 7–30 use ARIMA prediction.

💊 When to Fertilize

Enter your planted date to get a 30-day fertilizing schedule. Events are skipped on days with heavy rain, wet soil, or dry soil. Scaled to your land size.

🧪 When to Pesticide

Evaluates daily fungal, mite, and insect risk based on humidity, temperature, and rainfall. Only recommends spraying when risk is detected and conditions are safe. Product advice follows your farming method.

🌾 When to Harvest

Calculates your adjusted harvest date using actual weather stress since planting. Scores each day in the harvest window and alerts you if bad weather is approaching.

🚨 Crisis Mode

Monitors the next 6 days of real weather forecast and activates a system-wide alert when extreme conditions are detected. Affects all features when active.

🌿 My Crops

All crops you are currently growing or have harvested. Click a crop to use its data in other features.
